Ginseng

Common Names: Ginseng, Asian Ginseng, American Ginseng

Latin Names: Panax ginseng, Panax quinquefolius

Background

Ginseng is a slow-growing perennial plant known for its medicinal root, widely used in traditional Chinese and Korean medicine.

Uses

  • Boosts energy and reduces fatigue.
  • Enhances cognitive function and memory.
  • Supports immune health and overall vitality.
  • Used as an adaptogen to reduce stress.

Interesting Fact

The word "Panax" in its Latin name means "cure-all," reflecting its broad medicinal uses.
