Ginger

Common Names: Ginger

Latin Name: Zingiber officinale

Background

Ginger is a flowering plant whose rhizome (underground stem) is widely used as a spice and traditional medicine, especially in Asia.

Uses

  • Relieves nausea and morning sickness.
  • Reduces inflammation and muscle soreness.
  • Aids digestion and alleviates bloating.
  • Used in teas, food, and essential oils.

Interesting Fact

Ginger has been used for over 5,000 years in traditional Chinese and Indian medicine.
