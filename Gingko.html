Ginkgo

Common Names: Ginkgo, Maidenhair Tree

Latin Name: Ginkgo biloba

Background

Ginkgo is one of the oldest tree species in the world, known for its fan-shaped leaves and its use in traditional Chinese medicine.

Uses

  • Improves blood circulation and brain function.
  • Enhances memory and cognitive health.
  • Reduces symptoms of anxiety and dementia.
  • Used in teas, extracts, and supplements.

Interesting Fact

Ginkgo trees can live for over a thousand years, and some species have been around since the time of dinosaurs!
