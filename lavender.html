Lavender

Common Names: Lavender

Latin Names: Lavandula angustifolia

Background

Lavender is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region, known for its soothing fragrance and numerous health benefits.

Uses

  • Relieves stress and promotes relaxation.
  • Treats minor burns and insect bites.
  • Improves sleep quality.
  • Used in aromatherapy and skincare products.

Interesting Fact

Lavender oil is widely used as a natural remedy for anxiety and restlessness.
