Mint

Common Names: Mint, Peppermint, Spearmint

Latin Names: Mentha piperita, Mentha spicata

Background

Mint is a fragrant herb commonly grown in temperate regions. It is widely appreciated for its fresh aroma and culinary, medicinal, and cosmetic uses.

Uses

  • Aids in digestion and reduces nausea.
  • Provides relief from headaches and colds.
  • Used in mouth fresheners, teas, and essential oils.
  • Has antimicrobial properties for oral health.

Interesting Fact

Mint has been used for thousands of years, with traces found in ancient Egyptian tombs.
