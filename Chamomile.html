Chamomile

Common Names: Chamomile

Latin Names: Matricaria chamomilla, Chamaemelum nobile

Background

Chamomile is a daisy-like plant widely recognized for its calming effects and use in traditional herbal remedies and teas.

Uses

  • Promotes relaxation and improves sleep quality.
  • Soothes digestive issues and stomach discomfort.
  • Reduces inflammation and skin irritations.
  • Commonly consumed as tea or applied in skincare products.

Interesting Fact

Chamomile has been used as a natural remedy for over 2,000 years, and its essential oil is known for its anti-inflammatory properties.
