Echinacea

Common Names: Echinacea, Purple Coneflower

Latin Name: Echinacea purpurea

Background

Echinacea is a flowering plant native to North America, known for its vibrant purple petals and immune-boosting properties.

Uses

  • Enhances immune system function.
  • Helps prevent and treat colds and flu.
  • Supports wound healing and reduces inflammation.
  • Used in teas, tinctures, and supplements.

Interesting Fact

Echinacea was widely used by Native Americans for its medicinal benefits long before becoming popular in modern herbal medicine.
